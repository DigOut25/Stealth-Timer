FAQs

How do I record a custom sound?

Go to Settings → Round End Sound → Record New Sound. Tap the mic button to start recording, tap stop when done, preview it, then save with a name.

How do I use Gym Mode?

Start a session then tap the TV icon in the top right corner to switch to fullscreen display for mirroring to a TV.

My sound isn't playing

Close and reopen the app. Make sure your phone is not on silent mode.

How do I change the round end sound?

Go to Settings → Round End Sound and select from the preset sounds or record your own.
